Sokrates Media

A collection of images, logos and icons that you can use in sokrates reports, e.g., to provide an image for your custom tags tag.

You can refer to these images with the 

https://zeljkoobrenovic.github.io/sokrates-media/
prefix, for example, zeljkoobrenovic.github.io/sokrates-media/general/docker.png

Source code: github.com/zeljkoobrenovic/sokrates-media
